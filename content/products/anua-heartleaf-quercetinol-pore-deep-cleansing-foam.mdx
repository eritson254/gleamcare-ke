### What it does

This cleansing foam helps remove impurities and excess oil while leaving the skin feeling clean and refreshed. It’s especially suitable as the second step after an oil cleanser.

### How it fits in your routine

- **AM: Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect**
- **PM: Oil Cleanse → Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize**

### Quick tips

- Massage gently for 30–40 seconds for a thorough cleanse.
- Avoid over-cleansing if your skin feels tight.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Dispense a small amount and lather between hands.
3. Massage gently over the face.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Follow with toner and the rest of your routine.

**Patch test first** if your skin is sensitive.
