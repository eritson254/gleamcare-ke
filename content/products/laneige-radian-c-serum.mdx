### What it does

This serum works in the treatment step to support a smoother, more radiant-looking complexion. It layers easily under moisturizer and sunscreen.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Always apply sunscreen during the day when using brightening products.

### Quick tips

- Apply before heavier creams.
- Use consistently for best visible results.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply 1–2 pumps evenly across the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
