## Full description

My Way is a bright, modern floral: **bergamot and orange blossom** sparkle in the opening, then a creamy white-floral heart with **tuberose and jasmine**, finishing warm with **vanilla and cedarwood**.

## Sizes & pricing

- 20ml - 850 KES

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Add a small touch behind ears for a soft trail.
3. Reapply lightly if you want it brighter.

**Pro tip:** Layer with an unscented lotion to boost longevity.
