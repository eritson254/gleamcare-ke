## Full description

Coco Chanel leans **rich, spicy, and floral**, then settles into an **ambery, softly sweet base** that feels classic, feminine, and very polished.

## How to use

1. Apply on wrists and neck.
2. Start light and build slowly.
3. Let the dry-down come through before adding more.

**Pro tip:** Best when you want an elegant classic profile instead of something playful or overly sweet.
