## Full description

A Thousand Wishes smells like a **sweet, heart-warming celebration** with sparkling fruity notes up top, soft peonies in the middle, and a warm, creamy finish with **amber and amaretto creme** that feels cozy and feminine.

## How to use

1. Apply to pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears).
2. Layer with an unscented lotion to make the scent hold longer.
3. Reapply lightly as needed.

**Pro tip:** Add a tiny touch to clothing for a soft scent cloud.
