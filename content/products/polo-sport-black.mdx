## Full description

This profile opens crisp with **fresh citrus and an iced mango-style note**, then dries down into aromatic freshness and a smooth base built around **patchouli noir and woods**: clean, modern, and confident.

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Add a small touch to collar for extra trail.
3. Reapply lightly later if needed.

**Pro tip:** Works best when applied light; fresh notes stay cleaner.
