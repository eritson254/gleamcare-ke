## Full description

Drakkar Noir opens with **lavender and crisp herbal freshness**, then moves into a **spiced aromatic heart** and finishes with a **woody, mossy, slightly leathery base**. Strong, classic, and confident.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly (it's naturally bold).
2. Pulse points only: wrists and neck.
3. Let it develop before reapplying.

**Pro tip:** Great after showering; pairs well with clean grooming routines.
