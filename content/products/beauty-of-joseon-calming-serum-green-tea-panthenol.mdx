### What it does

This serum works as a calming treatment step to help reduce the look of redness and maintain a comfortable, balanced complexion.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Ideal for routines focused on soothing and barrier support.

### Quick tips

- Layer before heavier creams.
- Keep your routine simple if your skin feels irritated.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a small amount evenly across the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
