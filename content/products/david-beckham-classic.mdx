## Full description

David Beckham Classic opens with an **effervescent fresh burst** (often described as a fizzy "gin-tonic" style freshness), then dries down smooth into a **woody, fresh-amber** profile that's clean and easy to wear.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly to neck and wrists.
2. Add a tiny touch to your collar for more trail.
3. Reapply if you want the fresh opening to pop again.

**Pro tip:** Best when worn clean and light; don't over-apply.
