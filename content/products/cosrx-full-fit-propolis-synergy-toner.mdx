### What it does

This toner helps hydrate and prep the skin after cleansing while supporting a more radiant-looking complexion.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat (this toner) → Serum → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be layered for extra hydration.

### Quick tips

- Apply with your hands and gently pat in.
- Follow immediately with serum to seal in hydration.

## How to use

1. Cleanse your skin.
2. Dispense an appropriate amount into your palms.
3. Gently pat across the face until absorbed.
4. Follow with serum and moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
