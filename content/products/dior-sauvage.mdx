## How to use

1. Apply lightly on pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears).
2. For stronger projection, add a small touch to the collar area.
3. Reapply after several hours if you want a stronger presence.

**Pro tip:** Moisturize first; oils hold longer on hydrated skin.
