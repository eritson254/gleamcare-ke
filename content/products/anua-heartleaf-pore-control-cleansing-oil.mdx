### What it does

This cleansing oil works as your first cleanse to help dissolve makeup, sunscreen, and daily buildup. It leaves skin feeling clean and refreshed without a greasy finish.

### How it fits in your routine

- **PM: Oil Cleanse (this product) → Water-Based Cleanser → Treat → Moisturize**
- Use in the evening as part of a double-cleansing routine.

### Quick tips

- Always apply to dry skin first.
- Emulsify with water before rinsing for best results.

## How to use

1. Apply 1–2 pumps onto dry hands.
2. Massage onto dry face to dissolve makeup and buildup.
3. Add water to emulsify until milky.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Follow with a gentle foaming cleanser.

**Patch test first** if your skin is sensitive or reactive.
