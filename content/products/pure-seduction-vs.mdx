## Full description

Pure Seduction is a juicy fruity blend with **casaba melon and red plum** upfront, softened with **freesia/floral notes**, then finished with a clean, sheer **musk** feel.

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Layer with unscented lotion for longer wear.
3. Reapply anytime for a fresh fruity boost.

**Pro tip:** Apply right after showering; this profile stays brighter.
