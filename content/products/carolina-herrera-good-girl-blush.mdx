## Full description

Good Girl Blush opens bright with **bergamot and bitter almond**, then turns airy and floral with **peony and ylang-ylang**, finishing smooth and cozy with **vanilla and tonka bean**.

- 20ml - 850 KES

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Add a tiny touch behind ears for a soft trail.
3. Reapply lightly if you want more floral lift.

**Pro tip:** Layer with unscented lotion to make the base last longer.
