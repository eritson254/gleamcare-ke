This is a **warm, sweet gourmand**: caramelized vanilla up top, a nutty creamy middle, and a soft cozy finish that feels like dessert without being overwhelming.

## How to use

1. Apply to pulse points.
2. Layer with unscented lotion for extra longevity.
3. Use a tiny amount on clothing for a soft scent cloud.

**Pro tip:** This one shines at night, especially in cooler weather.
