## Full description

Aventus is known for its **fresh fruity kick** (think pineapple style brightness) and its **smoky woody dry-down** that feels clean, confident, and premium.

## How to use

1. Apply to neck and wrists.
2. Add a tiny touch to your shirt collar for extra projection.
3. Reapply lightly later if you want the fruitiness to pop again.

**Pro tip:** Don't over-apply; this one is best when it's smooth, not loud.
