### What it does

This sunscreen protects your skin from daily UV exposure while maintaining a comfortable, hydrated feel. It works well under makeup and does not leave a heavy finish.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (this sunscreen)**
- Always apply as the final step in your morning routine.

### Quick tips

- Apply generously for effective protection.
- Reapply every 2–3 hours when outdoors.
- Don’t forget the neck and ears.

## How to use

1. Complete your skincare routine.
2. Apply a generous amount evenly over face and neck.
3. Gently blend until fully absorbed.
4. Reapply during the day as needed.
