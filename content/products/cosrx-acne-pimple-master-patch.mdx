### What it does

These hydrocolloid patches help cover and protect active blemishes, reducing exposure to dirt and irritation while supporting a cleaner-looking healing process.

### How it fits in your routine

- Apply after cleansing and before heavier creams.
- Can be used during the day or overnight.

### Quick tips

- Apply to clean, dry skin.
- Do not layer serums or creams over the patch.
- Replace once the patch turns opaque.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and dry the affected area.
2. Apply the patch directly onto the blemish.
3. Leave on for several hours or overnight.
4. Remove gently and replace if needed.

For best results, avoid touching or picking at the area.
