Olympea mixes fresh florals with a distinctive **salt + vanilla** heart, finishing warm and smooth. It's sweet, clean, and confidently feminine.

## How to use

1. Apply to pulse points.
2. Keep it light during the day, add more at night.
3. Layer with an unscented lotion for extra hold.

**Pro tip:** This one gets better as it warms up on skin.
