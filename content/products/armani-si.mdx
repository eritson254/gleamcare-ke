## Full description

Armani Si is that confident elegant scent: **cassis (blackcurrant)** sweetness, soft florals, and a **smooth vanilla-woody** base that feels warm and sophisticated.

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Add a tiny touch behind ears for softness.
3. Reapply lightly if you're going out at night.

**Pro tip:** Works beautifully with a clean moisturizer underneath.
