### What it does

This sleeping mask helps replenish moisture overnight, supporting a smoother and more refreshed-looking complexion when you wake up.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat → Moisturize → Sleeping Mask (this product)**
- Use as the final step of your nighttime routine.

### Quick tips

- Apply 2–3 times per week or as needed.
- Use a thin, even layer.
- No need to rinse until morning.

## How to use

1. Complete your evening skincare routine.
2. Apply an even layer across the face.
3. Leave on overnight.
4. Cleanse as usual in the morning.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
