### What it does

This cleanser helps remove dirt and daily buildup while maintaining a soft, comfortable skin feel. It’s ideal for everyday use.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can also be used as the second step in double cleansing at night.

### Quick tips

- Massage gently for 30–40 seconds.
- Avoid hot water to prevent dryness.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Dispense a small amount and lather.
3. Massage gently over the face.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Continue with toner and the rest of your routine.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
