### What it does

This essence helps hydrate and support smoother-looking skin while improving the appearance of dullness. It works well as a lightweight treatment step before heavier serums or creams.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Essence (this product) → Serum → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be layered for extra hydration if needed.

### Quick tips

- Apply on slightly damp skin for better absorption.
- Use consistently for best results.

## How to use

1. Cleanse your skin.
2. Apply 1–2 pumps evenly across the face.
3. Gently pat until absorbed.
4. Follow with serum and moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
