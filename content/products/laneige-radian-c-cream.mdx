### What it does

This cream helps support a brighter-looking complexion while keeping skin hydrated and comfortable. It works well as your daily moisturizer in both morning and evening routines.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat → Moisturize (this cream) → Protect (AM sunscreen)**
- Always apply sunscreen in the morning when using brightening products.

### Quick tips

- Apply evenly across the face and neck.
- Pair with a hydrating serum for added glow.

## How to use

1. After serum, apply a small amount to the face.
2. Gently spread and press until absorbed.
3. In the morning, follow with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
