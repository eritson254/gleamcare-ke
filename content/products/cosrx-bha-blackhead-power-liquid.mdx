### What it does

This treatment works to gently exfoliate and improve the appearance of clogged pores and uneven texture. With consistent use, it supports a smoother-looking complexion.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat (this product) → Serum → Moisturize**
- Use sunscreen daily when incorporating exfoliating treatments.

### Quick tips

- Start 2–3 times per week.
- Avoid layering with other strong exfoliants in the same routine.
- Apply to dry skin for best results.

## How to use

1. Cleanse your skin thoroughly.
2. Apply a small amount evenly over affected areas.
3. Allow it to absorb before layering other products.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, apply sunscreen.

Patch test first, especially if your skin is sensitive.
