## Full description

Moon Sparkle is fruity, fun, and bright: **strawberry and juicy fruits** up top, a soft floral heart, then a smooth **musk-amber** dry-down that stays clean and pretty.

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Add a tiny touch to clothing for a soft scent trail.
3. Reapply for extra "sparkle" during the day.

**Pro tip:** Great layered with a light body lotion.
