### What it does

This serum helps replenish hydration and maintain a soft, comfortable skin feel. It works well under creams and sunscreen in daily routines.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Follow with a moisturizer to seal in hydration.

### Quick tips

- Apply on slightly damp skin.
- Layer twice if your skin feels extra dry.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply 1–2 pumps evenly across the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
