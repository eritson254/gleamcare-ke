### What it does

This cushion foundation helps even out skin tone while delivering a radiant finish. It offers buildable coverage that feels lightweight and comfortable.

### How it fits in your routine

- Apply after skincare and sunscreen.
- Use as your base makeup step.

### Quick tips

- Tap gently for a natural finish.
- Build layers gradually for more coverage.
- Set with powder if you prefer a longer-lasting result.

## How to use

1. Complete your skincare and sunscreen routine.
2. Press the puff into the cushion.
3. Gently tap across the face, blending evenly.
4. Reapply as needed for touch-ups.

Patch test if you have sensitive skin.
