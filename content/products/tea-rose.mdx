## Full description

Tea Rose is famous for smelling like **honest, authentic rose** with a soft lift of peony and chamomile, then a full rose bouquet (Tea Rose, Damask Rose, Bulgarian Rose) with a slightly green, clean finish from violet leaves and cedarwood.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly to wrists and neck.
2. Layer over lotion for a smoother, longer wear.
3. Reapply gently if you want the rose to stay bright.

**Pro tip:** Try it as a layering base under vanilla or clean musk scents.
