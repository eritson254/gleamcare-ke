Amber Wood is a modern woody-amber scent that starts with a **fresh spicy lift**, moves into a clean woody heart, and settles into a warm base built around **amber and patchouli**.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly to pulse points (wrists, neck).
2. Let it warm on skin before adding more.
3. Reapply only if you want stronger projection.

**Pro tip:** Apply after moisturizing; oils sit better and last longer.
