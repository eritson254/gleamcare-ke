## Full description

The One opens with a fresh-spicy feel, then turns warm and smooth, finishing with a **tobacco + amber style** base that feels grown and attractive.

## How to use

1. Apply to neck and wrists.
2. Add one small touch to the chest area for warmth.
3. Reapply lightly if you're going from day to night.

**Pro tip:** Best in the evening; this one becomes richer as it warms up.
