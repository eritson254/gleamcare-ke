### What it does

This serum works as a treatment step to support a brighter-looking complexion and improve the appearance of uneven tone.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Always apply sunscreen during the day when using vitamin C products.

### Quick tips

- Store in a cool, dry place.
- Start slowly if new to high-strength vitamin C.
- Avoid combining with multiple strong actives in the same routine.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a small amount evenly over the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
