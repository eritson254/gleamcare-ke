### What it does

This cleanser helps remove excess oil and daily buildup while supporting routines focused on clearer-looking skin. It’s ideal for oily and acne-prone skin types.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be used as the second step in a double cleanse at night.

### Quick tips

- If your skin feels dry, reduce usage to once daily.
- Use sunscreen during the day when using products with exfoliating ingredients.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Apply a small amount and gently massage into the skin.
3. Rinse thoroughly.
4. Follow with toner and the rest of your routine.

Patch test first, especially if your skin is sensitive.
