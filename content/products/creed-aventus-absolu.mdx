## Full description

Aventus Absolu opens with a vivid **grapefruit lift** and a bright **pineapple touch**, then turns warmer with spices before settling into a deeper base with **smoky vetiver and woods**.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly; this profile carries.
2. Neck + wrists is enough.
3. Reapply only if you want it louder.

**Pro tip:** Let it dry down for 10 minutes; the base is the best part.
