### What it does

This cleansing oil works as your first cleanse to break down makeup, sunscreen, and excess oil while maintaining a soft, comfortable skin feel.

### How it fits in your routine

- **PM: Oil Cleanse (this product) → Water-Based Cleanser → Treat → Moisturize**
- In the morning, you may skip oil cleansing unless needed.

### Quick tips

- Apply to dry skin first.
- Emulsify with water before rinsing.

## How to use

1. Apply 1–2 pumps onto dry hands.
2. Massage gently over dry face.
3. Add a little water to emulsify.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Follow with a foaming cleanser.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
