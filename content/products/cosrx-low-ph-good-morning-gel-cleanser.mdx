### What it does

This cleanser helps remove overnight buildup and daily impurities without disrupting the skin’s balance. It’s ideal as a gentle first step in any skincare routine.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can also be used as the second step in a double-cleansing routine at night.

### Quick tips

- Use lukewarm water to avoid dryness.
- Massage gently for 30–40 seconds before rinsing.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Apply a small amount and gently massage into the skin.
3. Rinse thoroughly.
4. Pat dry and continue with your routine.

Patch test first if your skin is highly sensitive.
