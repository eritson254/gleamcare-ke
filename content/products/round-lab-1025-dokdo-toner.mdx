### What it does

This toner helps refresh the skin after cleansing while supporting hydration and comfort. It prepares your skin for serums and moisturizers.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat (this toner) → Serum → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be layered if your skin feels dehydrated.

### Quick tips

- Apply with your hands for a gentler application.
- Follow immediately with serum to seal in hydration.

## How to use

1. Cleanse your skin.
2. Dispense a moderate amount into your palms or onto a cotton pad.
3. Gently pat or sweep over the face.
4. Follow with serum and moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
