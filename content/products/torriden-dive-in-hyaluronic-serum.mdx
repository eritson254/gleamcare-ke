### What it does

This serum helps replenish moisture and maintain a soft, plump-looking complexion. It fits seamlessly into both simple and multi-step routines.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Follow with moisturizer to seal in hydration.

### Quick tips

- Apply to slightly damp skin for enhanced hydration.
- Layer twice if your skin feels very dry.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply 1–2 pumps evenly across the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
