### What it does

This serum works as a treatment step to support a smoother, brighter-looking complexion while helping maintain a balanced feel on the skin.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Always apply sunscreen in the morning when using brightening products.

### Quick tips

- Start once daily if introducing niacinamide into your routine.
- Apply before heavier creams.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a few drops evenly across the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first, especially if your skin is sensitive.
