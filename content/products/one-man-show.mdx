## Full description

One Man Show is unapologetically classic with **green herbal aromatics** up front (basil/bergamot style), a spicy heart, and a deep dry-down that leans **woody, mossy, and masculine**. If you like bold "classic cologne" energy, this is that.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly (this profile can be strong).
2. Neck + wrists is enough.
3. Let it settle for 10 minutes before adding more.

**Pro tip:** Best at night or in cooler weather.
