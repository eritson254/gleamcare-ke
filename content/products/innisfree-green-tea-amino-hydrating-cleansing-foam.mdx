### What it does

This cleansing foam removes impurities while helping the skin feel soft and comfortable. It’s ideal for routines that prioritize hydration.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can also be used as the second step in a double cleanse at night.

### Quick tips

- Use lukewarm water to avoid dryness.
- Massage gently for 30–40 seconds before rinsing.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Dispense a small amount and lather between hands.
3. Gently massage over the face.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Continue with toner and the rest of your routine.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
