### What it does

This serum works as a targeted treatment step to help improve the look of breakouts and uneven texture. With consistent use, it supports a smoother-looking complexion.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize**
- Always use sunscreen in the morning when incorporating exfoliating products.

### Quick tips

- Start 2–3 times per week and increase gradually.
- Avoid combining with other strong exfoliating treatments.
- Apply to dry skin for best results.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a small amount evenly over the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. Use sunscreen during the day.

Patch test first, especially if your skin is sensitive.
