### What it does

This toner helps replenish hydration after cleansing and prepares the skin for the next steps in your routine.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat (this toner) → Serum → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be layered if your skin feels dry.

### Quick tips

- Apply with hands and gently pat into the skin.
- Use before thicker treatment products.

## How to use

1. Cleanse your skin.
2. Dispense a moderate amount into your palms.
3. Gently pat across the face until absorbed.
4. Follow with serum and moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
