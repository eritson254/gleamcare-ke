### What it does

This sunscreen stick provides daily UV protection with a matte finish, making it easy to reapply without disturbing makeup.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Morning: Cleanse → Treat → Moisturize → Sunscreen (liquid)**
- Use this stick for convenient reapplication during the day.

### Quick tips

- Apply generously for proper protection.
- Reapply every 2–3 hours when exposed to sunlight.

## How to use

1. As the final step of your morning routine, apply evenly across the face.
2. Reapply throughout the day as needed.
3. Glide gently over makeup without rubbing excessively.

Daily sunscreen use is essential, even on cloudy days.
