### What it does

This mineral powder helps control excess shine and set makeup for a smoother, longer-lasting finish.

### How it fits in your routine

- Apply after foundation or sunscreen.
- Use for touch-ups throughout the day.

### Quick tips

- Focus on oily areas like the T-zone.
- Apply lightly to avoid a cakey finish.

## How to use

1. After completing your base makeup, lightly dip a puff or brush into the powder.
2. Gently press or sweep onto oily areas.
3. Reapply as needed throughout the day.

Patch test if you have sensitive skin.
