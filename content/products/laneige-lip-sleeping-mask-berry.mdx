### What it does

This lip sleeping mask works overnight to help nourish and soften dry lips, leaving them feeling smoother and more comfortable by morning.

### How it fits in your routine

- Apply as the final step of your nighttime routine.
- Can also be used as a thicker daytime lip balm.

### Quick tips

- Use a small amount— a little goes a long way.
- Apply before bed for best results.

## How to use

1. Scoop a small amount using a clean applicator.
2. Apply evenly over the lips.
3. Leave on overnight.
4. Wipe or rinse off gently in the morning if needed.

Patch test if you have sensitive skin.
