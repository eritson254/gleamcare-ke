## Full description

Cafe Rose is a rich rose-forward scent with a warm, sensual edge-think **rose petals warmed by spice** and grounded by a smooth **amber-woody** base.

## Sizes & pricing

- 20ml - 850 KES
- 30ml - 1,170 KES

## How to use

1. Apply lightly (it wears more intense than a fresh floral).
2. Focus on wrists + one spot on the neck.
3. Let it bloom for 10 minutes before deciding to add more.

**Pro tip:** Best worn at night; rose + amber reads extra luxe in cooler air.
