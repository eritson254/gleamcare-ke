### What it does

This eye serum helps support smoother-looking skin around the eyes and improve the appearance of fine lines over time.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat (face serum) → Eye Serum (this product) → Moisturize**
- Best used in the evening.

### Quick tips

- Use a rice-sized amount for both eyes.
- Apply sunscreen daily when incorporating retinoid-based products.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and apply your regular serum.
2. Apply a small amount to the under-eye area.
3. Gently tap until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.

Patch test first, especially if you are new to retinal products.
