Pink Chiffon is that "pretty in pink" scent with sparkly fruity sweetness, soft florals, and a smooth **vanilla-orchid + chiffon musk** finish that stays light and wearable.

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Layer with lotion for longer wear.
3. Add a tiny touch to clothing for a soft scent cloud.

**Pro tip:** Sweet scents last longer on moisturized skin.
