## Full description

If your skin needs a richer feel, cocoa butter lotions are a classic. This one is ideal for keeping skin moisturized and smooth—especially after showering.

## How to use

1. Apply after showering to clean, dry skin.
2. Massage into dry areas (elbows, knees, legs).
3. Reapply where needed.

**Pro tip:** Apply at night for the softest morning feel.
