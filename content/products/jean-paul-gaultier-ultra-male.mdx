## Full description

Ultra Male opens sweet and juicy with **pear freshness**, adds **aromatic spice** through the middle, and dries down into a rich **vanilla-amber base** that feels bold, sweet, and youthful.

## Sizes & pricing

- 20ml - 850 KES

## How to use

1. Apply lightly because it projects well.
2. Focus on neck and wrists.
3. Let the sweetness settle before adding more.

**Pro tip:** This one works best when you want sweetness and projection, not subtlety.
