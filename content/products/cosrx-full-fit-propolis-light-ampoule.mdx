This ampoule works as a treatment step to help support hydration and improve the appearance of dullness, promoting a more radiant complexion.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this ampoule) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a few drops evenly across the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.
