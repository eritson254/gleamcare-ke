### What it does

This sunscreen helps protect your skin from daily UV exposure while maintaining hydration and comfort. It’s suitable for daily wear and layers well under makeup.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (this sunscreen)**
- Always apply as the final step in your morning routine.

### Quick tips

- Apply generously for full protection.
- Reapply every 2–3 hours when outdoors.

## How to use

1. Complete your skincare routine.
2. Apply an even, generous layer to the face and neck.
3. Gently blend until absorbed.
4. Reapply as needed during sun exposure.

Daily sunscreen use is essential, even on cloudy days.
