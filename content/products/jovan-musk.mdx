## Full description

Jovan Musk is a classic "clean musk" scent with fresh floral notes (jasmine/neroli/bergamot style) leading into a smooth musky heart, finishing warm with **sandalwood and tonka**. It's soft, wearable, and layers beautifully.

## Sizes & pricing

- 20ml - 850 KES
- 30ml - 1,170 KES
- 50ml - 2,000 KES

## How to use

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Keep it light for a clean skin-scent effect.
3. Layer under vanilla or floral scents for extra depth.

**Pro tip:** Moisturize first; musk notes cling better and last longer.
