## Full description

Polo Green is that **sharp, woody classic** with fresh green notes up top, then a rich, masculine dry-down with **leather, tobacco, oakmoss and patchouli** energy. It's bold, traditional, and memorable.

## How to use

1. Dab a small amount; this one can be strong.
2. Apply to neck and wrists.
3. Let it settle for a few minutes before adding more.

**Pro tip:** Best in the evening or cooler weather for maximum impact.
