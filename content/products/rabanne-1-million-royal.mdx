## Full description

1 Million Royal opens with **cardamom and bright citrus**, then shifts into an aromatic heart built around **lavender**, finishing with a smooth, warm base of **benzoin, cedar, and patchouli**.

## How to use

1. Apply lightly to neck and wrists.
2. Let it dry down before adding more.
3. Reapply only if you want stronger projection.

**Pro tip:** Best at night; keep it smooth, not loud.
