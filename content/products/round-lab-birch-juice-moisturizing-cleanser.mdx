### What it does

This cleanser removes impurities while helping maintain hydration and comfort. It’s ideal for dry or sensitive skin types.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can also be used as the second step in a double cleanse at night.

### Quick tips

- Use lukewarm water to avoid dryness.
- Follow immediately with toner or essence.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Dispense a small amount and gently massage into the skin.
3. Rinse thoroughly.
4. Pat dry and continue with your routine.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
