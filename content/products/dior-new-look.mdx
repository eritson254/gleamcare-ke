## Full description

New Look opens with an **"olfactory shock" of aldehydes** that feels bright, crisp, and modern, then reveals the sensuality of **frankincense** and a smooth **ambery accord** that stays warm and elegant.

## Sizes & pricing

- 20ml - 850 KES
- 30ml - 1,170 KES
- 50ml - 2,000 KES

## How to use

1. Apply lightly (aldehydes can project).
2. Pulse points only: wrists and neck.
3. Let it develop for a few minutes before adding more.

**Pro tip:** This profile wears best when it's airy; avoid over-applying.
