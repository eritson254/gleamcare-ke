### What it does

This cleanser helps remove excess oil and surface impurities while maintaining a fresh, comfortable skin feel. Ideal for oil-leaning routines.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be used as the second step in a double cleanse at night.

### Quick tips

- If skin feels tight, reduce usage to once daily.
- Always moisturize after cleansing.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Dispense a small amount and lather.
3. Massage gently over the face.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Continue with toner and the rest of your routine.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
