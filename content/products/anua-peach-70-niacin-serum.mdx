### What it does

This serum works in the treatment step to help improve the look of uneven tone and support a brighter, more refreshed complexion without feeling heavy on the skin.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Always use sunscreen in the morning when using brightening products.

### Quick tips

- Start once daily if new to niacinamide.
- Layer before thicker creams.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a few drops evenly over the face.
3. Gently press into the skin.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

**Patch test first**, especially if your skin is sensitive.
