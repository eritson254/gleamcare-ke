### What it does

This ampoule fits into your treatment step to help calm visible irritation and support a more balanced, comfortable skin feel—especially when your skin feels stressed.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this ampoule) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Can be used daily depending on your skin’s tolerance.

### Quick tips

- Apply on slightly damp skin for smoother application.
- Layer before thicker serums or creams.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply **2–3 drops** to the face.
3. Gently press into the skin until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

**Patch test first**, especially if your skin is highly sensitive.
