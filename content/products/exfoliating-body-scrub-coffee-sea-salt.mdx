## Full description

A good body scrub instantly upgrades your body care routine. Use it to smooth rough texture and help your lotions and oils absorb better.

### Where it works best

- Elbows and knees
- Back of arms
- Legs before body oil/glow routines

## How to use

1. Use on damp skin in the shower.
2. Massage gently in circular motions.
3. Rinse thoroughly and follow with lotion or body oil.

**Pro tip:** Don’t over-scrub—gentle pressure is enough for smooth results.
