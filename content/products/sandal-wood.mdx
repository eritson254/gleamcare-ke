## Full description

Sandalwood is loved for its **warm, woody, creamy** character with a soft musky sweetness. It wears like a smooth base note-grounding, elegant, and perfect for layering under florals, ambers, or vanilla scents.

## Sizes & pricing

- 20ml - 850 KES
- 30ml - 1,170 KES
- 50ml - 2,000 KES

## How to use

1. Apply to pulse points (wrists, neck).
2. For layering: apply Sandal Wood first, then your sweeter/floral scent on top.
3. Reapply lightly; this one lasts best when kept soft.

**Pro tip:** Pair with rose, vanilla, or amber scents for a richer dry-down.
