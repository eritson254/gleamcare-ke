Sweet Sunrise opens with **peach nectar and juicy mandarin**, then turns soft and floral (jasmine/gardenia vibes), finishing with a cozy gourmand base of **whipped vanilla and caramel**.

1. Apply to wrists and neck.
2. Layer with lotion for longer wear.
3. Reapply anytime for a sweet fruity boost.

**Pro tip:** This one smells best when it's soft; apply lightly.
