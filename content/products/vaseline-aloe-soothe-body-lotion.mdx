## Full description

A reliable everyday lotion that keeps your skin feeling comfortable and smooth. If you want simple, consistent moisture without overthinking it—this is it.

## How to use

1. Apply after showering to clean, dry skin.
2. Massage until absorbed.
3. Reapply to dry areas as needed.

**Pro tip:** For extra glow, apply a thin layer, then seal with a light body oil on top.
