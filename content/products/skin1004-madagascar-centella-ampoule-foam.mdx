### What it does

This cleanser removes dirt, excess oil, and buildup while helping maintain your skin’s comfort. It’s ideal as the first step in a routine focused on calm, balanced skin.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse (this product) → Treat → Moisturize → Protect (AM sunscreen)**
- Follow with toner or your treatment step while skin is slightly damp.

### Quick tips

- Use lukewarm water to avoid dryness.
- Massage gently for 30–40 seconds before rinsing.

## How to use

1. Wet your face with lukewarm water.
2. Dispense a small amount and lather between your hands.
3. Gently massage over the face in circular motions.
4. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry.
5. Follow with toner and the rest of your routine.

**Patch test first** if your skin is highly reactive.
