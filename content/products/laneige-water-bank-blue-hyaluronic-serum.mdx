### What it does

This serum helps boost hydration levels and supports a plumper-looking complexion. It fits seamlessly into both simple and multi-step routines.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Follow with a moisturizer to seal in hydration.

### Quick tips

- Apply on slightly damp skin for enhanced hydration.
- Use consistently for best results.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply 1–2 pumps evenly across the face.
3. Gently press into the skin until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
