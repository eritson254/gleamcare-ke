### What it does

This serum works in the treatment step to help balance excess oil and support a clearer-looking complexion. It is ideal for routines focused on shine control and visible congestion.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Treat (this serum) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Start once daily and adjust depending on your skin’s tolerance.

### Quick tips

- Avoid combining with multiple strong actives at once.
- Always apply sunscreen in the morning when using high-strength treatments.

## How to use

1. Cleanse and tone your skin.
2. Apply a small amount evenly over the face.
3. Gently press until absorbed.
4. Follow with moisturizer.
5. In the morning, finish with sunscreen.

Patch test first, especially if your skin is sensitive.
