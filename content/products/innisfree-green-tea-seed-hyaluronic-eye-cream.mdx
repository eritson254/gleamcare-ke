### What it does

This eye cream helps hydrate the delicate under-eye area and support a smoother, more refreshed look. It fits easily into daily routines.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Treat → Eye Cream (this product) → Moisturize → Protect (AM)**
- Apply before your main face cream.

### Quick tips

- Use a small amount for both eyes.
- Gently tap into the skin using your ring finger.

## How to use

1. After serum, apply a small amount to the under-eye area.
2. Gently tap until absorbed.
3. Follow with moisturizer.
4. In the morning, apply sunscreen to the rest of your face.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
