### What it does

This cleansing oil works as the first step in your evening routine, helping dissolve makeup, sunscreen, and daily buildup while leaving skin feeling soft.

### How it fits in your routine

- **PM: Oil Cleanse (this product) → Water-Based Cleanser → Treat → Moisturize**
- Optional in the morning unless needed.

### Quick tips

- Apply to dry skin first.
- Emulsify with water before rinsing thoroughly.

## How to use

1. Apply 1–2 pumps to dry hands.
2. Massage gently over dry face.
3. Add water to emulsify.
4. Rinse thoroughly.
5. Follow with a gentle foaming cleanser.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
