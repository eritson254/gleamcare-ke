### What it does

This bundle gives your skin hydration in layers. The toner preps and refreshes, the serum boosts hydration, and the cream helps lock everything in—supporting a softer, smoother-looking complexion without feeling heavy.

### How it fits in your routine

- **Cleanse → Toner → Serum → Cream → Protect (AM sunscreen)**
- Use **AM and PM** for best results.
- In the morning, always finish with **sunscreen**.

### Quick tips

- Apply on **slightly damp skin** for a more comfortable feel.
- Layer from **thinnest to thickest**.
- If you’re new to routines, keep it simple and stay consistent.

## How to use

1. **Cleanse** your face and pat skin slightly damp.
2. Apply the **Toner** and gently press into the skin.
3. Apply 1–2 pumps of the **Serum** and press until absorbed.
4. Finish with the **Cream** to lock in hydration.
5. **AM:** Apply sunscreen as your final step.

Patch test first if your skin is sensitive.
